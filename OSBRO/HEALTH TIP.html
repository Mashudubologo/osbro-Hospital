Heart disease is a leading cause of death. To protect your heart:

  • Eat fiber-rich foods like fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • Exercise 30 min/day, 5 times a week.
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • Get regular blood pressure and cholesterol checks.
