Our Services

General Medicine and Surgery +

Our general medicine department offers diagnosis, treatment, and preventive care for adults and children. Our surgery team provides modern surgical procedures using advanced medical equipment and sterile operating environments.

Pediatrics and Obstetrics +

We provide complete care for mothers, infants, and children. Our pediatricians specialize in vaccinations, child wellness, and early disease detection, while our obstetric team ensures safe deliveries and prenatal care.

Emergency and Outpatient Care +

Our 24-hour emergency department is equipped to handle critical cases efficiently. Outpatient services include consultations, minor procedures, and routine checkups, ensuring quality care with minimal waiting times.

Pharmacy and Laboratory Services +

Osbro Pharmacies dispense chronic and acute prescription medicine, over-the-counter products, and wound care items. Laboratory services provide accurate tests and diagnostic results to support doctors in treatment planning.

Radiology and Imaging +

Our radiology department uses advanced imaging technology such as X-rays, CT scans, and ultrasounds to assist in accurate diagnosis and monitoring of various health conditions.

Find a Pharmacy +

Osbro Pharmacies: Chronic & acute medicine dispensing, OTC medications, wound care products, and clinical pharmacy services.

Pharmacist Expertise: Safe medicine use, antibiotic management, and interaction analysis.

Focus: Optimizing treatment, reducing risks, and minimizing waste.

Clicks Pharmacy Network: 36 locations at Medicross centers and 44 at Netcare pharmacies offering vitamins, supplements, and first aid products.

Family Connect Line +

Our Family Connect Line helps you stay informed about your loved ones admitted to Osbro Hospital. With visitor restrictions in place, families can receive updates and support from hospital representatives or social workers.
